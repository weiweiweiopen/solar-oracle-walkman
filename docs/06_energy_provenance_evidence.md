# Energy Provenance Evidence

Energy provenance matters because researchers, builders, and the public need transparent ways to reason about where measured energy/material behavior came from and how it was recorded.

## What this project can currently claim
- It provides a V1 prototype workflow for validating and storing IV voiceprint-like data.
- It demonstrates oracle-signed report verification and on-chain record insertion.
- It begins a public research registry pattern for documenting measurement events.

## What this project cannot currently claim
- It does not claim legal REC status.
- It does not claim T-REC status.
- It does not claim energy equivalence.
- It does not claim a production-grade certification system.
- It does not claim a financial product.

In this repository, the phrase **Energy Provenance Evidence** refers to research-oriented evidence records, not legal certification.
