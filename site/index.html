Three-minute explainer

The oracle speaks from evidence, not certainty.

A patterned DSSC is illuminated, swept across voltage, and recorded as current values. The curve becomes a material trace: not a legal certificate, not a universal hardware identity, but a structured record of how one visible photovoltaic object responded under stated conditions.

Security boundary

A co-factor, not a root of trust.

DSSC response may strengthen a provenance story by adding material liveness and event freshness evidence. Formal authentication still requires calibrated measurement hardware, replay-resistant protocol design, and secure signing through a secure element or server-side key management.

  • No frontend API keys, wallet collection, or private signing keys.
  • No fake minting, fake transaction, or fake certificate flow.
  • No claim of legal REC, T-REC, D-REC, carbon credit, energy equivalence, or financial product status.
