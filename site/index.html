Public research prototype

Solar Oracle Walkman

This is an experiment in how DIY solar panels can develop unique identities at the semiconductor level. To make the result more intuitive for people without a scientific background, the identity of each handmade solar cell—its I–V curve—is translated into unique generative music. We are also building a mobile Walkman version of this system. Ultimately, the experiment asks how independent, distributed green certificates and energy trading might become possible—and I believe it is also connected to future open compute systems.
